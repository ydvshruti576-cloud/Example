Our Affiliations & Approvals
We are recognized and affiliated with leading academic and professional bodies in India.

CBSE Affiliated

Recognized by Central Board of Secondary Education (Delhi) for our school till Class 12.

ICSE / ISC Affiliated

Affiliated with CISCE for both ICSE (Class 10) and ISC (Class 12) examinations.

PCI Approved

Pharmacy Council of India approval for B.Pharm, D.Pharm and M.Pharm programs.

Affiliated to AKTU

Approved by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University for B.Pharm, M.Pharm and Management programs.

Affiliated to CSJMU

Affiliated with Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University, Kanpur for B.Ed and other UG programs.

Approved by BTE

Diploma courses approved by Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh for D.Pharm and more.

AICTE Approved

Technical courses like BCA, BBA and MBA approved by AICTE for professional excellence.

NCTE Approved

B.Ed program approved by National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).
